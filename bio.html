Laura S. Hofmann

Computer Science & Cognitive and Brain Sciences, Tufts '16

Though originally from Germany/ the UK, I am currently a junior at Tufts University, double majoring in Computer Science and Cognitive & Brain Sciences. My recent interests include interface design and human computer interaction research that bridges my two fields of academic study. When not busily coding away, I enjoy adventuring in the great outdoors, either out on the water as a member of Tufts' Varsity Rowing team, or up in the mountains as a Wilderness Orientation Leader and as Gear Director of the Tufts Mountain Club.
